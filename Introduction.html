The Impact of AI on the Workforce

As it replaces the people who once did the job themselves, Artificial Intelligence has already taken over the workforce by a storm. Nowadays, the humans are building such robots to take over the jobs of other humans to increase efficiency, lower costs, increase revenue, and so forth.
The implementation of AI may be taking over jobs, but new ones are still being created. It is mostly taking over jobs with repetitive tasks and jobs with tasks that requires an individual to go through plenty of data, but they still cannot take over jobs that involve problem-solving, leadership role, and creativity.
