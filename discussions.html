Discussion

Artificial Intelligence brings multiple advantages to humans in multiple areas. It reduces human errors, increasing the chance of reaching accuracy. AI can do risky things which may endanger humans, like defusing bombs. It is available for 24/7 and it also helps humans with repetitive jobs like document verification in banks as humans will get very bored of repeating the same tasks every day. Digital assistance like Chatbots saves the need for human resources as artificial intelligence and automation are handling them.
AI takes decisions faster compared to humans who analyse factors both emotionally and practically. When humans play Chess games in Windows, it is impossible to beat the system in hard mode due to the AI behind the game. AI also has daily applications like Apple’s Siri and Google’s Alexa which simplifies a human’s daily routine like searching for information and replying to messages.

However, artificial intelligence also brings disadvantages like high costs of creation for software and hardware of complex machines. They make humans lazy as the inventions are automating a majority of human’s work. AI increases the unemployment rate as they are replacing the minimum qualified individuals with AI robots. They are lacking high order thinking skills because they work only based on their algorithms developed by humans.
Nevertheless, human workforce brings a lot of advantages. It relieves the burden of multiple business companies because human workforce departments will get involved in time-consuming tasks which will enable the higher position workers to focus on the big-picture items like gaining market share. Other than that, the human workforce helps to maintain compliance as they possess the expertise in areas such as discriminatory laws, compensation laws.
The human workforce also has disadvantages like relinquishing control regarding how the business operates. This can cause the employees to end up making decisions that are not in the organization’s best interests.
The human workforce also increases the time and money usage. This is because a business will have to take some time to hire the staff for the department and they represent a significant cost to the business depending on how large the human workforce department is.

Based on the objective of the report, it is concluded that artificial intelligence will not replace the human workforce because they will both function together with each other. Humans will not be able to depend on artificial intelligence or human workforce completely as they both have their weaknesses. However, when both are combined, it increases perfection in the task given and they will be able to complete an activity given at the best quality in minimum time using minimal resources.
For example, during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, humans were unable to control the spread of the disease due to the risk of them getting infected and the low number of human workforces. So, artificial intelligence has been brought in to work together with the human workforce to reduce the spread of the disease. Temperature checking and disease testing were some of the activities where both the human workforce and artificial intelligence were actively involved.
