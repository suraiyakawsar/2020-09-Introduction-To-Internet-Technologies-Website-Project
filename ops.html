Objectives
The focus of our research is based on these main objectives:
  1. Is Artificial Intelligence going to replace human labour?
  2. Will the introduction of AI affect the availability of jobs to a significant degree in most sectors?
  3. Does automation destroy or create jobs?
  4. How far are we from a future where the owners are the only humans associated with a company?
Problem Statements What Needs To Be Done
  • The implementation of AI can trigger substantial job cuts.
  • Companies need to balance out their method of implementing AI into their business.
  • The rate of unemployment will increase since there might be millions of people unable to get a job because they could not meet the requirements.
  • We need to reform our entire education system to provide more opportunities for people to concentrate on certain skills.
  • We need to raise both public and private investments so that they are better aligned with market demand.
  • We need to change the conditions of the labour market by bridging the supply-demand gap and enhancing the gig economy.
  • A gradual transition to AI has already begun in several sectors, and they have started replacing employees. Due to which, people have developed a fear of being disempowered.
  • People need to understand how AI will affect future labour demands in any way so that the proper implementation of AI is successful and in the process of which, AI is able to create new jobs and new employment categories.
